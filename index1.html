Benefits of Becoming Lead

Leadership skills:

Becoming a leader can help students develop important leadership skills that can be valuable in their future careers.

Networking :

Club leaders have the opportunity to meet and connect with other students who share their interests, as well as potential partners and sponsors

Resume Building:

Being a club leader can be an impressive addition to a student's resume, demonstrating their leadership abilities, organizational skills, and commitment to a particular cause

Personal growth :

Leading a club can be a challenging but rewarding experience that can help students develop confidence, independence, and a sense of purpose

Making a difference:

By leading a club, you will have the opportunity to make a positive impact on their campus and community, bringing people together around a shared interest or goal.

Certificates:

Get Certified from the best which is recognised by government of India

What a Lead should have?

Passion:

Look for candidates who are passionate about the subject or activity that the club is centered around. This passion will help motivate them to put in the time and effort necessary to lead the club effectively

Communication skills:

Leaders need to be able to effectively communicate with club members, other leaders, and potential partners. They should be able to convey information clearly and concisely and be open to feedback from others.

Organizational skills

Leaders need to be able to plan events, manage budgets, and delegate tacks effectively. They should be able to stay on top of deadlines and ensure that everything runs smoothly.

Creativity

Leaders should be able to think outside of the box and come up with creative solutions to problems. This can help make the club more engaging and attract new members

Flexibility

Leaders should be able to adapt to changing circumstances and be open to new ideas. This can help the club stay relevant and meet the needs of its members.
